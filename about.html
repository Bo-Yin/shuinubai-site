關於我 About

筆名：水糯白
性別：她
居住地：台灣

喜歡看BL小說的畫家，ACG方面，專攻二次元人物動漫插畫，有很長時間是畫厚塗，正在轉型成清透的電繪水彩風格。對ACG以外的插畫也有高度興趣，主攻簡筆人物插畫，SD比例人物插畫。


Pen Name: Shui Nuobai
Pronoun: She/her
Location: Taiwan
I speak Chinese and English.

An artist who loves reading BL novels, specializing in anime and manga character illustrations in the ACG field. For a long time, she used thick, heavy-painting techniques, but is currently transitioning to a clearer digital watercolor style. She also has a strong interest in illustration outside of ACG, focusing on simple line drawing and SD-scale character illustrations.
