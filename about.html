關於我 About

筆名：水糯白
居住地：台灣

熱愛BL的畫家。我喜歡把文字角色具象化成鮮明的圖像，讓想像力擁有更多視覺的原料。我喜歡創造美型的角色，同時我還希望能讓觀者能感受到角色的情緒氛圍，像是溫柔、思念、孤獨等等——那些角色所散發出的情感能量。


Pen Name: Shui Nuobai
Location: Taiwan
English as second language.

I'm an artist who loves BL. I enjoy turning written characters into vivid illustrations, feeding the imagination with more visual material. I like drawing beautiful characters, but more than that, I hope to convey their emotional states to the viewer — such as gentleness, longing, loneliness, and other feelings that radiate from within them.


巴哈姆特
